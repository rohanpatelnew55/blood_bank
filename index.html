What we do?

Blood Case Management

Blood for Nepal team is actively managing blood cases. Many patient parties face difficulties in finding donors in time.

Awareness for Pregnant Women

Educating on managing blood supply before childbirth and advising the arrangement of at least four donors in advance.

Blood Donation Program

To ensure blood availability, we organize donation programs across the country. This addresses situations where blood banks or donors are unavailable in time.

Blood Donation Awareness

We promote blood donation and educate people on its benefits. We also provide strategies for managing blood needs, especially for operations and deliveries.
